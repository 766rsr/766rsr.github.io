3D Gamming

By Chih-Hsuan, Wang and Tze-Shin, Chen

#NYCU #Unity

In an ancient continent shrouded in mystery and magic, a young adventurer begins to notice the world's many injustices. Driven by a desire for truth and justice, the protagonist embarks on a quest to unearth the continent's hidden stories. Each encounter on this journey is not only a test but an opportunity for growth, honing skills and enhancing equipment. As the adventure progresses, the ultimate goal becomes clear: to reveal the dark truth lurking behind the continent's facade. The key to this mystery is a formidable and malevolent sorcerer, a master of dark arts who guards the world's secrets. This sorcerer, with his sinister plans, poses the greatest challenge. It is up to the young adventurer to thwart this evil, confront the sorcerer, and restore order to the world, thus fulfilling a destiny greater than any imagined.
